Related Works

Brian and his band members have made some great music through their years. Here is a list of all of the albums in order:

1. "Sounding of The Seventh Trumpet" - (Thraser music)

2. "Waking the Fallen" - (Longer songs with improved playing, first album to include solos)

3. "City of Evil" - (Crazy guitar playing)

4. "Avenged Sevenfold" "White Album" - (Uses a lot of brass and other stringed instruments)

5. "Live in the LBC" "Diamonds in the Rough" - (Combination of covers from other bands and re-doing some older songs)

6. "Nightmare" - (Dedicated to there former drummer "The Rev" or James Sullivan)

7. "FoREVer" - (Also dedicated to James Sullivan)

8. "Hail to the King" - (Songs with a gritty tone)

9. "Hail to the King: Deathbat" - (Mostly themes of other songs)

10. "Best of 2005-2013" - (Songs from City of Evil - FoREVer)

11. "The Stage" - (Really crazy songs)

12. Black Reign" - (Songs made for the game "Call of Duty: Black Ops 4")
