In the Beginning

Brian Haner Jr. or "Synyster Gates" as his stage name was born on July 7, 1981 in Long Beach, California. In 1999, him and his 4 other buddies liked to played music and decided to be a band. But before that, him and the drummer where already in a band through 2001-2002 called Pinkly Smooth. When they left Pinkly Smooth and realized that their friends already had a band called "Avenged Sevenfold". After that band they left and came back to help the band with there first album called "Sounding Of The Seventh Trumpet". As a kid him and his friends listened to a lot of rock and metal. Some people include: Slash, Dimebag Darrell, John Petrucci, Steve Vai, and many more. He also likes to listen and play to classical and jazz music. He gets most of this from his father "Brian Haner Sr." which is a musician that likes to play classic and jazz music.

Brian's Career

Over the past 20 years, the band has made it big out in the world of hard rock. Some people would consider them "metal" back in their early days. In total, they have 12 albums to date and they all have done very well in the charts even breaking some records of other albums over the years. In 2008, Brian and Zacky "the rhythm guitarist" won the award of "best guitarists" of the year. Brians net worth is roughly $16 million which is really good for a musician. He is really good at what he does and will keep doing this in the future. He even has his own website dedicated to help people play guitar and showing his own methods.
